Buzz Clock is a simple, discrete way to tell the time — even when you can't take your phone out of your pocket. In interviews, on first dates, in church, or at school plays, Buzz Clock silently vibrates at set intervals, so you'll always know what time it is.

Instructions

To use Buzz Clock, just set an “Interval” and tap “Done” to return to the main screen. Your phone will now vibrate at that interval, with three fast, attention-getting buzzes followed by a number of slow buzzes indicating the time. For instance, with the “Interval” set to “15 Minutes,” half-past the hour would be announced with three fast buzzes followed by two slow buzzes. Three fast buzzes alone mark the top of the hour.

You can press the Sleep/Wake button on the top of your phone to blank the screen — or simply wait a few minutes for it to blank itself — and Buzz Clock will continue to work. As long as the program is running, you'll continue to get buzzed.

Troubleshooting

I'm not getting buzzed. Why?
You need to have your iPhone set to allow vibration, controlled via the Setup ▶ Sounds settings.

Depending on the position of your Silent/Ring switch — the small, two-position switch on the side of your phone, next to the volume control — your phone may not be able to vibrate. Change the settings as appropriate, and Buzz Clock will act as expected.
